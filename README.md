# CPO Intelligence Wiki — an LLM Wiki for Chief Product Officers

A personal knowledge system that compounds everything a CPO knows — competitive intel, customer insights, product decisions, delivery tracking, and team performance — into a living, interlinked wiki maintained by Claude.

Inspired by [Andrej Karpathy's LLM Wiki pattern](https://gist.github.com/karpathy/442a6bf555914893e9891c11519de94f). Adapted for the CPO context: sources are customer calls, analyst reports, sprint summaries, and win/loss notes. The wiki grows richer with every ingest. Nothing gets re-derived from scratch.

**How it works:** paste the prompt into Claude Code, open Obsidian alongside it, and say Start. Claude reads your wiki, orients itself, and is ready to ingest sources or answer questions. You bring the signals. Claude does the maintenance.

**Use cases covered:**
- Competitive intelligence
- Customer insights accumulation
- Product decision memory
- Product development & delivery tracking
- Team & product performance

**Stack**: Claude Code + Obsidian
